Emmanuel Aboah Boateng is currently an Applied Scientist at Microsoft, where his focus is on the development and application of Generative AI/LLMs to address pressing real-world challenges. Before joining Microsoft, he obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering at the Tennessee Technological University in Cookeville, Tennessee, USA, specializing in artificial intelligence and data provenance analysis techniques for anomaly detection. He has leveraged his knowledge and skills in AI via internship experiences in tech firms such as Apple Inc., Meta Platforms Inc., and BBB Industries, LLC.

His current research interests broadly cover Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Anomaly Detection for cyber-physical systems security. With a keen interest in solving complex mathematical problems and making accurate predictions, he thrives on using a diverse range of machine learning techniques and data analysis tools. Additionally, Emmanuel's experience extends to signal processing, microprocessor design, and computer architecture, further enhancing his ability to tackle multifaceted challenges.
