Emmanuel Aboah Boateng is currently pursuing his Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Tennessee Technological University in Cookeville, Tennessee. He specializes in developing artificial intelligence frameworks and data provenance analysis techniques for anomaly detection in hardware/embedded devices and cyber-physical systems. He is interested in solving complex mathematical problems and making predictions using various machine learning algorithms and data analysis. He is also experienced with digital signal processing, VLSI physical design, microprocessor design, and computer architecture.
He is a detail-oriented, responsible, and resourceful engineer with a get-it done, on-time and high quality outcome spirit.
